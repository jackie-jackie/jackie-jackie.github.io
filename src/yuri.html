Poccha Climb!

I'm usually not a big fan of sports manga but I love "Pocha Climb!". It doesn't stretch out tournaments or makes them overly dramatic. No endless flashbacks and tragic backstories. No comically evil rivals. Just girls having fun bouldering, and most importantly, a proper romance arc.

Real shame it got axed :(
